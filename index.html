Our Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

To transform scholars' voices into tangible victories, ensuring that every scholar's potential is nurtured, their concerns are addressed with urgency, and their contributions are celebrated as the cornerstone of a thriving academic community.

Mission

The Voice of Scholars (VoS) is passionately dedicated to amplifying the diverse voices of scholars, transforming their concerns, aspirations, and everyday challenges into meaningful action that fosters an inclusive, equitable, and empowering environment.

How ?

This will be instrumentalized through the establishment of dynamic platforms, advocacy for practically progressive change, and frontal spearheading of impactful projects that ensure every scholar feels listened to, valued, amply supported, and potently empowered to thrive academically, socially, economically, and personally.

Our Manifesto

1

Advocating for Reasonable Living Conditions

Voice of Scholars champions dignified, student-centered living standards for all scholars – this should include access to reliable Wi-Fi, safe and comfortable accommodation, and the autonomy to choose or change roommates when necessary. Every scholar, as punctuated by the 'scholars' handbook,' deserves a conducive environment that supports their academic journey and well-being.

"Just because you've not experienced the problem; it doesn't mean that the problem doesn't exist!"

2

Revitalizing Extra-Academic Support Systems

Extra-academic support is deeply rooted in the Voice of Scholars vibrations. We commit to working with the program team to revive the financial support that has, for years now, catapulted many scholars into pursuing paths that add meaning to their career paths. The support was cut recently and efforts to resuscitate it have not borne fruit.

This leadership is dedicated to its renaissance to bring the support back at the scholars' disposal. Be it extra transport programs, skill-building initiatives, and non-academic support avenues that nurture the whole scholar.

3

Recognizing and Celebrating Scholar Excellence

To sustain and enhance scholars' motivation, leadership development, and academic stamina, it is imperative to institute recurring recognition initiatives. Periodic events and platforms honoring scholars' commitment are essential to building an environment that galvanizes their drive to pursue excellence and reject complacency.

Voice of Scholars will work tightly with the program team and the program director to institute consistent recognition events such as award ceremonies and digital platforms to spotlight scholars' accomplishments in domains of leadership, academic, innovation, and service to the community.

4

Establishing a 'Scholars-First' Rapid Response Squad

Every one in three scholars will report stories of the program team dragging their feet on issues that feel like life-or-death priorities. Whether it's a one-time headache or a recurring nightmare, many scholars have been there, and it's frustrating.

We promise to work hand-in-hand with the program team to make sure this squad is ready to roll, cutting through red tape to fix issues like delayed stipends, visa snags, or travel hiccups before they excruciate us. Our goal? To make sure every scholar feels heard and supported, no more chasing answers or stressing over stuff that is out of control.

5

Fostering Scholar Solidarity – On Campus and Beyond

As Voice of Scholars (VoS), we are all committed to bringing scholars together like one big, unstoppable family – whether you're grinding on campus or out there making waves as an alum. Our goal is to build a tight-knit community where everyone feels connected, supported, and ready to lift each other up.

This will see the creation of inclusive programs that spark real bonds, mentorship networks that drop wisdom and open doors, and ongoing engagement that keeps the Scholar-Connect vibes strong no matter where life takes you. We want to take the Program's Scholar Connect vision beyond the August break entertainments.

Why? Because a crew that has got each other's backs isn't just surviving – it's thriving.
